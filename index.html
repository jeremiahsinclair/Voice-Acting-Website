About

I am a Non-Union, African American voice actor with a deep, lifelong passion for the craft. My fascination with voice acting began at 11 years old, when playing Telltale’s The Walking Dead for the first time opened my eyes to the medium, sparking a deep admiration for the legendary Dave Fennoy.

Years later, around the age of 23, my official journey began when a beautiful twist of fate brought me into contact with Fennoy's cousin, who graciously connected us for a personal conversation. Taking that unforgettable moment as a definitive sign from the universe, I committed myself to rigorous professional training and took my acting career to the next level. I haven't looked back since.

Operating from a custom, broadcast-ready home studio, I deliver professional, pristine audio files with rapid turnaround for animation, video games, and commercial projects worldwide.
