About

I am a Non-Union, African American voice actor with a deep, lifelong passion for the craft. My fascination with acting started when I was just 7 years old, and nothing brings me more joy than getting behind the microphone and bringing unique characters to life.

Operating from a custom, broadcast-ready home studio setup, I provide professional, pristine audio files with a rapid turnaround time for animation, video games, and commercial projects worldwide.
